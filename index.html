Sydney Night Safety

The data featured on the maps below was created from a paper mapping activity at our pop-ups on the 21st of March; one in the afternoon at Catigal Green and the other in the evening outside of Newtown Station.

We asked people to place a sticker on a paper map to mark areas they feel safe or unsafe at night. We had 96 responses, here they are, mapped.
